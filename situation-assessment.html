Situation Assessment Call

Clarity before commitment.

A standing invitation

They have a decision to make, but not yet a service to buy.

Most clients come to us at a point where clarity matters more than speed. Whether it's a job offer, a family member's permit, or a future application, this is about gaining clarity on your options before taking the next step.

01
What this is

A focused, one-hour advisory session.

You bring your situation — your plans, timelines, family context, and the questions that aren't easily answered through official guidance. We work through it with you and provide a clear, experience-based view of your options.

We will cover:

  • The permit routes realistically available to you
  • Options that may not be viable — and why
  • Key trade-offs, risks, and timelines
  • A practical recommendation based on your situation

This is not a sales call. If a full-service engagement makes sense, we'll say so. If you can confidently proceed on your own, we'll tell you that too.

02
What you receive

A clear, practical way forward.

  • A structured discussion tailored to your situation
  • Clear guidance on viable permit pathways
  • A practical, experience-led recommendation
  • A concise written summary within 48 hours

04
Scope

What's included, and what isn't.

Included

  • In-depth discussion of your situation
  • Identification of relevant permit options
  • Timeline and strategic considerations
  • Direct, honest guidance

Not included

  • Document review or formal legal assessment
  • Application drafting or submission
  • Representation before authorities
  • Ongoing case management
  • Outcome guarantees
