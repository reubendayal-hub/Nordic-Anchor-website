+

What's Included

  • Salary compliance check against current scheme thresholds
  • Full application preparation and SIRI submission
  • Advice on the Job Change Rule — when the candidate may legally start and how to manage the transition
  • Case tracking until the new permit is issued

SIRI Government Fee

DKK 6,810 — paid directly to SIRI, separate from Nordic Anchor's fee.

The Job Change Rule

Under SIRI regulations, the candidate may begin working at your company on the same day the new application is submitted — provided they already hold a valid Danish work permit from their previous employer and it has not yet expired. If they start before submitting, or their permit has expired, they must stop work until the new permit is approved.

Important

Do not let the candidate start work before their new permit application is submitted to SIRI — this creates a compliance risk for your company. We will manage the submission as quickly as possible to minimise any gap in their start date.
